📋 Image Guidelines

  • Stand upright facing the camera.
  • Capture your complete body from head to feet.
  • Keep the camera straight (avoid tilted photos).
  • Hold a reference card (Credit Card / Debit Card / PAN Card) horizontally.
  • Keep the card close to your body around waist or chest level.
  • Keep your other arm stretched away from the body.
  • Wear fitted clothing for better accuracy.
  • Ensure good lighting with minimal shadows.
  • Avoid mirrors and multiple people in the frame.
  • Do not crop your feet or head.
